Here's the 411

Where is Camp PIxEL?

Camp PIxEL is beginning at Aliso Viejo Christian School in Southern California. We are looking to expand the program to elementary schools all over the place. If you are interested, please shoot us an email!

When does Camp PIxEL happen?

Camp PIxEL is happening on five days, from July 13th (Monday) to July 17th (Friday), and begins at 12:00 PM and ends at 3:00 PM every day. On the last day, we will have a 45-minute Demo Day where we will showcase the students' work for parents and faculty!

How much does Camp cost?

We are charging $115 for Camp PIxEL, with online payments, to cover the costs of web hosting, domain registrations, and other special things for your students which they will receive upon the first day of Camp!

Who are you?

PIxEL (which stands for Programmers + Innovators * Entrepreneurs + Leaders) is run and organized by the PIxEL Foundation, a pending 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that directs programs for code, design, and business. If you are interested in partnering with us, please send us an email.
